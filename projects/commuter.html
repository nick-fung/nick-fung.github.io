Diary of a Commuter

Motivation and Goals

For the first couple months of the school, I've been commuting to and from UBC by bike; after all, it's only a twelve minute bike ride. Then it started raining. And then it started getting cold. That made me wonder - how does the weather affect my travel patterns? What about my travel time to school? The goals of this project - which I've yet to decide a name for - is primarily to learn the basics of web development, as I have no prior experience and I thought it would be fun to do over Winter break. Additionally, as I haven't had any stats or data analytics courses, this project makes lets me kill two birds with one stone.
