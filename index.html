nwHacks 2018

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an event where teams of 2-5 people collaborate on an application within the specified time limit (nwHacks is 24 hours long). Prizes are awarded to the most creative, polished and theme-specific projects or “hacks”. The winners are chosen by a panel of judges comprised of industry professionals.

About nwHacks

nwHacks is Western Canada's largest hackathon, with over 600 student hackers coming from throughout North America. nwHacks is a student led iniative, and is run by a group of 20 students from UBC.

Our team is devoted to fostering creativity, as well as giving students the opportunity to network within the tech community. Over the course of 2 days, hackers can expect a variety of great food, fun activities, informative workshops held by our sponsors, and much much more.
